About Doc&I

Doc&I supports the mission of Health Care Providers and Covered Entities to provide better care for their patients. The Doc & I solution combines patent-pending technology with on premise expertise that works personally with staff and patients to maximize 340B program savings and patient care efforts.

Our prescription transparency software, built for healthcare providers, ensures patients receive the best value for their prescriptions prior to arriving at the pharmacy.

We integrate data from multiple sources using a proprietary database and algorithm to deliver point-of-care, real-time prescription pricing and pharmacy benefit information to healthcare providers within their existing workflow.

Our Mission

At Doc&I it is our mission to ensure patients receive the best value for their prescription(s) by empowering doctors and patients with transparent medication and pharmacy choices at the point-of-care.
